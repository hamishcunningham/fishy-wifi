Water Temperature Sensor

The water temperature sensor is fully submersible and should be placed in the fish tank.

Internally, it is a DS18B20 1-wire temperature sensor (datasheet), packaged in a stainless steel tube and sealed against water. It has three connections, typically coloured black, red and yellow for ground, power and data respectively.

The waterelf connector for this sensor is a three pin type SP-13, with numbered pins on the plugs and sockets themselves. The diagram below is looking into the face of the sensor plug.

Pin Function Cable colour
1 Ground Black
2 Power (3.3V) Red
3 Data Yellow
